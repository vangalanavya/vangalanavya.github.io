About Me

PhD researcher in Seed Science and Technology with 5+ years of research experience in seed quality enhancement using nanotechnology.

Selected as an Indo-German Bilateral Fellow (2023) - Top 20 among 8,000 participants across India, gaining international exposure at prestigious institutions in Germany and the Netherlands including Max Planck Institute, Wageningen University, and KWS SAAT SE.

My research focuses on developing nutrient-doped carbon nanodots for agricultural applications, achieving 30% improvement in seed germination under stress conditions. This technology is currently being tested for commercialization with seed companies.
