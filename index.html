About Me

I am a passionate agricultural scientist dedicated to revolutionizing seed technology through innovative nanotechnology applications. With 5+ years of research experience, I strive to develop sustainable solutions that enhance crop productivity and support farmers worldwide.

My journey in agricultural research began at Dr. PDKV Akola, where I graduated as a Top 2 Academic Performer. This foundation led me to specialize in Seed Science and Technology, culminating in my current doctoral research at PJTSAU, Hyderabad.

In 2023, I was honored to be selected as an Indo-German Bilateral Fellow - one of only 20 students chosen from over 8,000 applicants across India. This prestigious fellowship provided me invaluable exposure to cutting-edge research at Max Planck Institute, Wageningen University, and KWS SAAT SE in Germany and the Netherlands.

My research on carbon nanodots has achieved 30% improvement in seed germination under stress conditions, with technology now being commercialized with leading seed companies. I am committed to bridging the gap between laboratory innovations and real-world agricultural applications.
